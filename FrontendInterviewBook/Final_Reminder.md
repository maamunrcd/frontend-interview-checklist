# Final Reminder — Day of Interview

One short page: what to do, what to remember, and a quick confidence boost. You've prepared; trust the process.

---

## What Interviewers Look For

**Not:** Someone who knows everything or codes perfectly first try.

**Yes:** Clear thinking · Problem-solving · Communication · Willingness to learn · Collaboration · Production awareness · Passion for craft.

---

## Do & Don't

**Do:** Ask clarifying questions · Think out loud · Start simple · Test with examples · Discuss trade-offs · Say when you're stuck · Stay calm.

**Don't:** Jump to code without planning · Stay silent · Ignore edge cases · Give up · Panic.

---

## On Interview Day

1. **Sleep well** (7–8 hours) · **Eat well** · **Join early** (5–10 min).
2. **Stay calm** — you've got this.
3. **Be yourself** — authenticity and enthusiasm matter.
4. **Ask questions** — shows engagement.
5. **Have fun** — it's a conversation, not an interrogation.

---

## Remember

> "The goal isn't to know everything. It's to demonstrate **problem-solving**, **technical knowledge**, and **communication**."

> "You don't need to be perfect. You need to be **competent**, **collaborative**, and **curious**."

> "Every interview is practice. Learn, improve, and keep going."

---

**Good luck. You're ready. Go ace it.**
